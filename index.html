About Our Club

Kopong Table Tennis Club is a vibrant community sports club dedicated to promoting table tennis in Kopong and surrounding areas. As an affiliated member of the Botswana Table Tennis Association, we provide professional training and competitive opportunities for players of all ages.

Our primary focus is grassroots development, working closely with local primary schools to identify and nurture young talent. We believe in the power of sport to transform lives and build character in young people.

Through our dedicated coaching program, we aim to develop not just skilled players, but confident and disciplined individuals who value teamwork, respect, and determination.
